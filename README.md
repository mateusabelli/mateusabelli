## About Me

I'm a Software Engineer and Open Source enthusiast from Brazil 🇧🇷.

I enjoy exploring creative ways to develop projects and to have fun while learning new things in the process. I also like to work on ideas and build them into complete solutions.

To craft high quality software, I believe that it should to be meaningful, helpful, teach something along the way, and/or just be fun to do it. Otherwise, I'd just be a machine, much inferior to the other ones out there.
